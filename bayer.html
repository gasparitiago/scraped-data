Unleashing the Power of Alpha for HER2-expressing Tumors
Targeted Thorium Conjugates (TTC) are an emerging class of targeted alpha radiotherapy that delivers alpha radiation directly to tumors inside the body. By targeting HER2, our investigational HER2-TTC may offer a novel, powerful tool for treating a wide range of aggressive tumor types.
