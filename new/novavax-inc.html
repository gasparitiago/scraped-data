Our pipeline

Pipelinecreating tomorrow's vaccines today

The world urgently needs new tools to help solve some of our greatest infectious disease challenges. Novavax is applying our unique technology to address current and emerging threats.

Novavax continually conducts clinical trials, investigations, gathering of real-world evidence, and development of vaccines to address public health threats. Our scientists in our research and development (R&D) programs are committed to the efficient delivery of new vaccines while maintaining the safety of patients who take part in our trials.
